Parker Herren

Writer. Podcaster. Dancer. Pilates Instructor.

Parker Herren is a freelance journalist, podcaster, dancer, and Pilates instructor based in New York.

Originally from north Texas, Parker earned a BFA in Modern Dance from Texas Christian University. Since moving to New York City, he has worked as a Pilates instructor while performing with various Modern Dance companies, notably Amy Marshall Dance Company. Parker is the creator, host, and producer of the podcast What’s Your Favorite Scary Movie and is currently a student at CUNY’s Craig Newmark Graduate School of Journalism.
